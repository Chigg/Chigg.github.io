Colin Higgins's personal blog

I've made this website to show off some of the neat things I've been up to.
Expect apolitical assessement of interesting journal articles, simple code tutorials, and personal projects.

Sociology and Computer Science may seem like widely different topics that don't have much in common... but they intersect in very topical and interesting ways. Consider the apps you use and the websites you visit. How much of an effect do these tools have on your life, even inadvertly? Although typically thought of as a way to generate ad revenue, imagine the societal insights we could generate using data about usage patterns and demographs! I'm incredibly passionate about both fields, and I'd love to share that passion with you.

I believe the intersection of Sociology and Computer Science has a massively topical importance in our society. Through the projects I'll contribute to this blog, I intend to help substantiate that claim and hopefully garner a similar passion in you, the reader.

To stay up to date on my contributions, please sign up for the email list below. I don't have a regularly planned schedule for updates, so they'll come infrequently. I promise not to share your details or spam you with emails.
